What this map is — and isn’t
The surface blends each planning area’s score into a smooth gradient for readability — values come from area statistics, softly feathered at the edges (≈300 m), not from a physical model. The index combines exposure (PET, UTCI, night-time cooling, 2022), sensitivity (age structure 2025, social indicators 2023) and coping capacity (street trees, green space, fountain distance, 2025). Weights are documented choices, not objective truth. Area averages don’t describe any individual. The map points to where help should arrive first — it is not a label on any neighbourhood.
